Steps of Cooking Ugali

Ordered list

  1. Place Sufuria on source of heat
  2. Add water to the Sufuria
  3. Heat water to boiling point of about 100 degrees
  4. Cooking oil
  5. Add at least 2 cups of maize flour
  6. Start Stirring till a paste is formed
  7. Continue adding Flour till paste hardens
  8. Start Shapping to desired shape
  9. Leave for approximately 10 minutes before turning
  10. When ready overturn it onto a plate
  11. Serve with desired Stew
